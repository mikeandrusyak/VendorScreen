# Privacy Policy — VendorScreen AI

**Effective date:** 2026-07-03
**Last updated:** 2026-07-03

This Privacy Policy explains how the **VendorScreen AI** application ("**Application**"), operated by `Andrusiak Mykhailo`, an independent individual developer ("**we**", "**us**"), processes data when installed on a monday.com account. The Application is a business-to-business tool. In most cases the Customer (the organization installing the Application) is the **data controller** and we act as a **data processor** on the Customer's behalf.

---

## 1. Data We Process

| Category | Examples | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Vendor screening input** | Vendor / counterparty name submitted for screening (may include the name of an individual, e.g. a sole trader) | monday.com board item, via webhook |
| **monday.com identifiers** | Board ID, item (pulse) ID, column IDs | monday.com webhook payload |
| **Authentication tokens** | Short-lived monday.com session token (JWT), signing secret | monday.com |
| **Operational logs** | Timestamps, risk level produced, error messages | Generated by the Application |

We do **not** intentionally collect special-category (sensitive) personal data. The vendor name you submit may, depending on your data, relate to an identifiable individual — you are responsible for the lawfulness of submitting it (see Section 6).

## 2. How We Use Data

- To perform the requested sanctions/PEP screening of the vendor name.
- To write the resulting risk indicator and notes back to your monday.com board.
- To operate, secure, debug, and maintain the Application.

We do **not** sell your data, use it for advertising, or use it to build profiles for any purpose other than the screening you request.

## 3. Third-Party Data Sharing (IMPORTANT)

To perform screening, the Application transmits the **vendor name** to **OpenSanctions** (`https://api.opensanctions.org`), a third-party sanctions and PEP data provider. This transfer is necessary to deliver the Service. OpenSanctions processes the query under its own terms and privacy policy. No monday.com item content other than the vendor name is sent to OpenSanctions.

The Application runs within **monday.com's serverless environment (monday code)**; board updates are sent to the **monday.com API**. We do not transfer your data to any other third party except as required by law.

## 4. Data Retention

- **Screening inputs are processed transiently** and are not stored in a persistent database by the Application.
- Results are written back to **your** monday.com board and are thereafter under your control and retention policy.
- Operational logs may be retained for a limited period for security and debugging, then discarded.

## 5. Data Security

- Communication with monday.com and OpenSanctions occurs over **HTTPS/TLS**.
- Webhook requests are authenticated using monday.com's **signing secret (JWT verification)**.
- The Application uses **short-lived, per-account session tokens** for board updates in production.
- Secrets are stored as environment variables within the monday code environment and are not committed to source control.

## 6. Lawful Basis and Customer Obligations

Where the Customer is the data controller, the Customer is responsible for establishing a lawful basis (e.g. legitimate interest or legal obligation for AML/KYC) for processing vendor data through the Application, and for providing any required notices to data subjects. By using the Application, the Customer instructs us to process the submitted data solely to provide the Service.

## 7. International Transfers

OpenSanctions and monday.com may process data outside your country. Where applicable, such transfers rely on the safeguards offered by those providers. You are responsible for assessing the adequacy of these transfers for your own compliance requirements.

## 8. Data Subject Rights

Because we act as a processor and do not maintain an independent store of screening inputs, requests to access, correct, or delete personal data should be directed to the **Customer** as controller. We will assist the Customer in responding to such requests as required by applicable law.

## 9. Children's Data

The Application is a B2B tool and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly process the personal data of children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date and, where required, through the marketplace listing.
